Feedback is the mechanism by which deviation is reduced within declared tolerance thresholds.

  

Alignment defines direction.

  

Tolerance defines acceptable deviation.

  

Integrity defines structural coherence.

  

Feedback enforces contraction of variance under constraint.

  

Without feedback, probabilistic synthesis may expand.

  

With disciplined feedback, variance tends to compress.

  

Feedback is not commentary.

  

It is structured constraint injection.

The difference is scope.

  

Reinjection reasserts agreement.

  

Feedback targets deviation.
